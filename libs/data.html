balance_outlier(X, y, e=0.1)[source]

Resample X according to epsilon such that n_outlier = e * n_inlier

Parameters:

  • X (data)

  • y (labels must be in [1, 0], with 1 being inliers and 0 being outliers)

  • e (epsilon to use for outlier sampling. The number of outliers will be equal to e * n_inlier.)
